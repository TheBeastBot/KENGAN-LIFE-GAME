# Dragon Ball Sourced VFX And Audio

These non-character, non-card-art assets are sourced from Kenney CC0 packs and are used only for combat VFX sprites and sound effects.

## Sources

- Kenney Particle Pack: https://kenney.nl/assets/particle-pack
- Kenney Impact Sounds: https://kenney.nl/assets/impact-sounds
- Kenney Sci-fi Sounds: https://kenney.nl/assets/sci-fi-sounds
